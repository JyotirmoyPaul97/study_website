A modern, all-in-one study platform designed to streamline learning for students from 8th to 12th standard.
This project focuses on organization, productivity, and performance tracking—because studying shouldn’t feel chaotic.

🚀 Features
📖 Lectures Management
Access and organize subject-wise lectures
Structured learning flow
📝 Study Content
Notes, PDFs, and important materials in one place
📌 Assignments
Track and submit assignments easily
Stay ahead, not last-minute panic
📅 Daily Tasks
Plan your day with structured study goals
Build discipline over time
⏰ Reminders
Never miss deadlines, classes, or tests
🧠 Tests & Quizzes
MCQs and assessments for practice
Improve accuracy and speed
📊 Student Progress Tracking
Performance analytics
Identify strengths and weak areas
